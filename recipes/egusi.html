Egusi soup

EGUSI SOUP

Ground melon seeds slow-cooked in palm oil with assorted meat, stockfish, and crayfish. Deep, earthy, and rich — it coats every swallow like it was made for it. The kind of soup that tastes better the next day.

Ingredients

  • Ground melon seeds
  • Palm oil
  • Assorted meat
  • Stockfish
  • Dried fish
  • Crayfish
  • Locust Beans(iru)
  • onion
  • pepper
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Ugwu(or Bitter leaf)
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Boil assorted meat and stockfish with chopped onions, seasoning cubes and salt. Boil till tender. Set aside the stock.
  2. Blend pepper and remaining onions together. Set aside.
  3. Heat some palm oil in a pot, Mix grounded egusi with little water into a thick paste, then scoop into hot oil in chunks. Fry for 8-10minutes.
  4. Pour in pepper and stock. Leave to boil on medium heat for about 10 minutes.
  5. Add meat and fish, and other protein.
  6. Add ugwu(or bitter leaf) and salt to taste.
  7. Serve with accompaniment of choice.
